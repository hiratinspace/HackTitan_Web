Lunch + Company Booths Open

Free catered lunch. Chat with Google, Anthropic, Stripe, AWS, Microsoft & GitHub reps.

7:00 PM
Dinner

Late-Night Snacks + Mentor Office Hours — Round 2

FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before you show up to build.

hacktitan@iwu.edu Join our Discord
Any current college student is welcome. You don't need a CS background — designers, pre-med, business students all thrive at HackTitan. High school students may join with a mentor's approval.
Teams can be 1–4 people. Solo hacking is allowed and there's even a special prize for it. We'll have team formation during opening ceremonies if you need teammates.
100% free to attend. That includes all meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner x2 + snacks), swag, and all workshops. We don't charge a dime.
Your laptop, charger, any hardware you want to hack with, a sleeping bag if you're staying overnight, and your best ideas. We'll provide everything else.
Brainstorming is fine. No code can be written before the start gun. All code must be written during the 24-hour window — judges verify via git history.
Projects are scored on: Innovation (30%), Technical Depth (25%), Impact (25%), and Presentation (20%). Each track has a dedicated panel of industry judges.
